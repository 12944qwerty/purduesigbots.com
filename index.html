Purdue ACM SIGBots is Purdue University's Vex U robotics team. The team participates every year in the Vex U Robotics Competition and has traveled to the Vex Robotics World Championships every year that the club has been in existence. The team is established as a Special Interest Group (SIG) for Purdue's chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), and is considered to be a part of Purdue's Computer Science department.
